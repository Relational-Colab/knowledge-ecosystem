# Digital Ethics Foundations / 数字伦理基础
## Overview / 概述
Digital Ethics examines how technology affects people, society, and institutions.
It provides a framework for evaluating the moral, cultural, and governance implications of digital systems.

数字伦理研究技术如何影响个人、社会和机构。
它为评估数字系统的道德、文化和治理影响提供框架。

This page introduces the core concepts that form the foundation of the Digital Ethics domain and course bundle.

本页介绍构成数字伦理领域和课程基础的核心概念。

## Core Concepts / 核心概念
* Privacy / 隐私
Protecting personal information and respecting boundaries around data collection and use.
保护个人信息，尊重数据收集和使用的边界。

* Consent / 同意
Ensuring individuals understand and agree to how their data and interactions are used.
确保个人理解并同意其数据和互动的使用方式。

* Transparency / 透明性
Making system operations, decisions, and data practices understandable and visible.
使系统操作、决策和数据实践变得可理解、可见。

* Bias / 偏见
Recognizing and mitigating unfair patterns in data, algorithms, or human decision‑making.
识别并减轻数据、算法或人为决策中的不公平模式。

* Fairness / 公平性
Ensuring systems treat individuals and groups equitably.
确保系统对个人和群体进行公平对待。

* Accountability / 问责
Assigning responsibility for system outcomes and ensuring mechanisms exist to address harm.
为系统结果分配责任，并确保存在处理伤害的机制。

* Governance / 治理
Establishing rules, oversight, and processes to ensure ethical operation of digital systems.
建立规则、监督和流程，以确保数字系统的伦理运行。

## Why Digital Ethics Matters / 数字伦理的重要性
Digital systems shape decisions about:
* access
* opportunity
* identity
* rights
* safety
* social participation

数字系统影响以下方面的决策：

访问

机会

身份

权利

安全

社会参与

Ethical considerations ensure technology aligns with human values and societal expectations.

伦理考量确保技术与人类价值观和社会期望保持一致。

## Cultural Anchors / 文化锚点
Digital Ethics aligns with both Western and Chinese traditions:

Western: rights, autonomy, accountability

Chinese: 和谐 (harmony), 责任 (responsibility), 公正 (justice), 秩序 (order)

数字伦理与西方和中国传统均有契合点：

西方：权利、自主、问责

中国：和谐、责任、公正、秩序

These anchors help learners connect abstract ethical concepts to familiar cultural frameworks.

这些锚点帮助学习者将抽象的伦理概念与熟悉的文化框架联系起来。

# Domains and Courses: Dual‑Identity Architecture / 领域与课程的双重身份架构
## Overview / 概述
This page explains how the knowledge‑ecosystem uses a dual‑identity model to organize and deliver content.
Domains provide the semantic knowledge structure, while courses provide the pedagogical structure.
Both identities coexist, reinforce each other, and share the same underlying artifacts.

本页解释知识生态系统如何使用双重身份模型来组织和呈现内容。
领域（Domain）提供语义知识结构，课程（Course）提供教学结构。
两者共存、互相强化，并共享相同的基础内容。

## 1. Domains as Knowledge Structures / 领域：知识结构
Domains represent coherent clusters of related concepts. They define the semantic architecture of the knowledge‑ecosystem.

领域代表一组相关概念的连贯集合，定义了知识生态系统的语义架构。

### Characteristics / 特点
* Organize concepts by meaning and relationships
* Provide stable, reusable knowledge units
* Support multilingual glossary entries
* Enable cross‑domain linking
* Serve as the foundation for teaching materials

按意义和关系组织概念

提供稳定、可复用的知识单元

支持多语言术语表

支持跨领域链接

为教学材料提供基础

### Examples / 示例
* Systems Foundations / 系统基础
* Digital Ethics / 数字伦理
* AI Fundamentals / 人工智能基础

## 2. Courses as Pedagogical Structures / 课程：教学结构
Courses represent structured learning pathways built on top of domains. They define the instructional flow and learning progression.

课程是在领域之上构建的结构化学习路径，定义了教学流程和学习进程。

### Characteristics / 特点
* Provide learning sequences
* Integrate cultural anchors and examples
* Use domain concepts as building blocks
* Support bilingual teaching
* Adapt knowledge for specific audiences

提供学习顺序

融入文化锚点和示例

使用领域概念作为构建模块

支持双语教学

为特定受众调整知识

### Examples / 示例
* Systems Analysis & Design / 系统分析与设计
* Digital Ethics / 数字伦理课程

## 3. Dual Identity: How Domains and Courses Interact / 双重身份：领域与课程如何交互
Domains and courses coexist in a layered architecture.

领域与课程在分层架构中共存。

### Domains Provide / 领域提供
* Conceptual clarity
* Semantic stability
* Multilingual terminology
* Cross‑language scaffolding

概念清晰度

语义稳定性

多语言术语

跨语言支架

### Courses Provide / 课程提供
* Pedagogical sequencing
* Teaching context
* Cultural framing
* Learning pathways

教学顺序

教学语境

文化框架

学习路径

### Shared Artifacts / 共享内容
* Bilingual glossary entries
* Teaching pages
* Topic overview pages
* Lookup indexes

双语术语文件

教学页面

主题概览页面

索引文件

## 4. Architectural Representation / 架构呈现
/i18n/
Holds multilingual term files representing domain knowledge.
存放表示领域知识的多语言术语文件。

/pages/
Contains teaching pages and topic bundles representing course structures.
存放教学页面和主题包，代表课程结构。

/docs/
Provides contributor and governance documentation.
存放贡献者指南和治理文档。

## 5. Benefits of the Dual‑Identity Model / 双重身份模型的优势
* Reusability across teaching, consulting, and self‑study
* Clear separation of knowledge and pedagogy
* Scalable multilingual architecture
* Contributor‑friendly structure
* Strong alignment with governance principles

在教学、咨询和自学中高度复用

清晰区分知识与教学

可扩展的多语言架构

对贡献者友好

与治理原则高度一致

## Summary / 总结
Domains define what we know.
Courses define how we teach it.

领域定义“我们知道什么”。
课程定义“我们如何教授它”。

The knowledge‑ecosystem integrates both identities to create a flexible, multilingual, and pedagogically rich environment.

知识生态系统整合这两种身份，构建灵活、多语言、教学丰富的环境。
